Privacy Policy – Emu

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Emu (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Emu collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Emu mobile application and related services.

Emu is an AI-based stock analysis and market intelligence platform. Emu does not provide trading services, investment advice, or execution capabilities.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Phone number or email address (for login and account identification)
  • Basic profile identifiers (user ID)

We do not collect unnecessary personal details such as address, date of birth, or government IDs.

b) Subscription and Billing Information

Subscriptions are handled entirely by:

  • Google Play Billing (Android)
  • Apple In-App Purchases (iOS)

We do not collect or store:

  • Credit/debit card numbers
  • UPI details
  • Bank account information

We only receive:

  • Subscription status
  • Plan type (Free / Pro / Premium)
  • Expiry and renewal timestamps
  • Purchase verification tokens (used only for validation)

c) Usage and Interaction Data

To improve Emu’s analysis and reliability, we collect limited usage data such as:

  • Tickers viewed or analyzed
  • Feature usage (e.g., Intraday Trend, TempoCast, Ask Emu)
  • Session timestamps
  • App performance metrics (crashes, errors)

This data is not sold and is used strictly for product improvement and system stability.

d) Ask Emu (Conversational AI)

When you use Ask Emu, we process:

  • Your questions
  • Context related to the stock or analysis you are viewing

Ask Emu responses are generated using Emu’s internal analytical data. We do not use Ask Emu conversations to:

  • Build advertising profiles
  • Train third-party AI models
  • Sell or share personal conversations

Conversations may be temporarily stored to improve response quality and debugging, then purged according to retention policies.

e) Device & Technical Data

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App version
  • Anonymous identifiers (for analytics and crash reporting)

This data cannot be used to personally identify you.

2. What Emu Does NOT Collect

To be very clear, Emu does not:

  • Access your contacts
  • Access your photos, files, or media
  • Track your location
  • Read SMS or call logs
  • Execute trades or access broker accounts
  • Collect biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data only to:

  • Provide stock analysis and insights
  • Unlock features based on subscription plan
  • Improve accuracy, stability, and performance
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or misuse
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure with restricted access
  • Industry-standard security practices are followed
  • Access is limited to essential system operations

No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

Data may be shared only with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (for hosting and computation)
  • App stores (Google / Apple) for billing verification
  • Legal authorities, if required by law

All sharing is limited to what is strictly necessary.

6. Analytics and Third-Party Services

We may use analytics tools to understand app usage trends and improve reliability. These tools receive only anonymized or aggregated data and do not receive personal financial or trading information.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information
  • Cancel your subscription via the app store
  • Stop using Emu at any time

To delete your account or request data removal, contact us at: emuanalyzersupport@gmail.com

8. Children’s Privacy

Emu is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Financial Disclaimer (Important)

Emu provides analytical insights, not investment advice.

  • All scores, probabilities, and projections are informational
  • Past performance does not guarantee future results
  • Users are responsible for their own financial decisions

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of Emu implies acceptance of the updated policy.
